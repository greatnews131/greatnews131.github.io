Global and International App

The well-organized, highest rated (4.7 star ★), smart news aggregator app with user-centered design

World News app provides latest news feeds, topbuzz and updates in different news categories like Breaking News & Top Stories, Politics, Business, Technology, Gaming, Sports, Entertainment & Arts, Lifestyle, Travel, Health, Cryptocurrencies and Region-based news feeds (US news, UK news, Canada news, Europe news, Asia news, Africa news, Middle East news etc.) from unbiased, local & international news sources from all around the world.

World News app brings you fresh news in a fast and easy way to read, share with others & save for your future reference. You can also use our powerful RSS feed reader to follow your favourite news feed sources.

World News app is the completely free news app for all the users for a lifetime.
